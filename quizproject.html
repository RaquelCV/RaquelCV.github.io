Personality Quiz

One of the very first coding projects I created in my Computer Science class was a personality quiz with the help of Natalie Berrum. The quiz is a simple personality quiz that asks you questions and tells you what type of milkshake you are. The quiz is made up of 10 simple questions, with each having 5 options. The results you can get are chocolate, strawberry, vanilla, mint, and cookies and cream.Very simple and fun.
Through this project I was able to learn more about coding with Processing as well as what it takes to work with a partner when coding. One of the the biggest struggles with creating this quiz was being able to merge all of our work.
